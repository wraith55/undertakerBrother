Dawn of the Deadman


In 1990, the Undertaker debuted at the Survivor Series pay-per- view show, as the mystery partner for the Million Dollar Man’s “Million Dollar Team.” The Undertaker, standing nearly 7 feet tall and over 300 pounds, was immediately a force to be reckoned with. He soon began feuding with the World Wrestling Federations top superstars, such as the Ultimate Warrior, Yokozuna, and most notably, Hulk Hogan, whom he defeated for his first world heavyweight title, just one year after his initial debut.
